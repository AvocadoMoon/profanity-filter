These are the instructions:
- A: to create the English reference profanity word list, which is used to create the profanity word lists for all other languages
- B: to create the profanity word lists for all other languages using the English reference profanity word list


A) Creation of the English reference profanity word list

Starting point:
- list of English profanity wordlist (without scores): wordlist_original.en
- list of Italian profanity wordlist (without scores): wordlist_original.it
- the clean parallel corpus English-Italian
- a parameter THR say (0.30)

Step 1:
Using `wordlist_original.it`, compute the profanity scores of `wordlist_original.en` and store the output in `wordlist_full.en`
`java -cp /opt/profanity-filter/target/profanity-filter-1.0.jar com.modernmt.text.profanity.dictionary.DictionaryBuilder data/clean/en__it wordlist_full.en wordlist_original.it wordlist_full.en &> /dev/null`

Step 2:
Select the English entries in `wordlist_full.en`, whose profanity score is larger than the threshold THR, and store them into `wordlist_THR.en`
`cat wordlist_full.en | awk -F'\t' -vthr=THR '{if ($2 >= thr) {print $1}}' > wordlist_THR.en`



B) Creation of the  profanity word list for language XX
Starting point:
- list of English profanity wordlist (with scores): wordlist_THR.en
- list of Italian profanity wordlist (without scores): wordlist_original.XX
- the clean parallel corpus English-XX
- the parameter THR say (0.30)

Step 1 for :
Using the reduced English list `wordlist_THR.en`, compute the profanity scores of `wordlist_original.XX` and store the output in `wordlist_en020.XX`
`java -cp /opt/profanity-filter/target/profanity-filter-1.0.jar com.modernmt.text.profanity.dictionary.DictionaryBuilder data/clean/en__XX wordlist_original.XX wordlist_THR.en wordlist_enTHR.XX &> /dev/null`

Step 2:
Select the entries in `wordlist_enTHR.XX`, whose profanity score is larger than the threshold THR, and store them into `wordlist_enTHR.XX`
`cat wordlist_enTHR.XX | awk -F'\t' -vthr=THR '{if ($2 >= thr) {print $1}}' > wordlist_enTHR_THR.XX`
